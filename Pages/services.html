FAQ

Each system can be customized to meet the specific needs of businesses or residential properties, ensuring comprehensive coverage and peace of mind.
How long does the installation process take?
The installation timeline varies based on the size and complexity of the project. For small residential setups, installation might take a few hours, while larger commercial systems could require several days. We conduct a thorough assessment beforehand to provide an accurate timeline tailored to your needs.
Can I monitor my security system remotely?
Yes! Our systems are compatible with remote monitoring via smartphones, tablets, or computers. This allows you to view live camera feeds, receive real-time alerts, and manage your security system from anywhere with an internet connection.
What kind of maintenance and support do you provide after installation?
We offer ongoing maintenance and support services to ensure your system remains in optimal working condition. This includes regular system checks, software updates, and troubleshooting assistance. Our support team is available to address any questions or issues that may arise after installation.
